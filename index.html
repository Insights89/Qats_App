Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Qats ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

This App is intended for entertainment purposes only.

1. Age Restriction

Our App is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use this App.

2. Data Transmitted to the Developer

We do not transmit any user data to our own private servers or custom backends.

  • Local Storage Only: Your high scores, sound settings, and music settings are managed locally on your device via the GameData class using LibGDX Preferences. This data is not synced to the cloud.
  • No Analytics: We do not use custom analytics integrations (such as Firebase Analytics or Flurry) to report or track your usage behavior.

3. Data Transmitted to Third Parties (Advertising)

We use Unity Ads to provide advertisements within the App. Because we have integrated the Unity Ads SDK, your device transmits certain data to Unity (a third-party service). According to Unity's standard practices, this data typically includes:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Google Advertising ID (AAID) or Android ID.
  • Device Metadata: Device model, OS version, screen resolution, and battery/network status.
  • Technical Data: IP address (used for approximate geographic location like city or country).
  • Ad Interaction: Data on when ads are shown, clicked, or when a rewarded video is completed.

You can learn more about how Unity handles your data by visiting the Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Entertainment Purpose

This App is provided solely for entertainment purposes. Any actions taken within the game are virtual and have no real-world monetary value or consequence.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
